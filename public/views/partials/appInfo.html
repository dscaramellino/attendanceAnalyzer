About Attendance Analyzer

User Story:

This application is a teacher/principal facing dashboard that allows the user:

  1. To easily monitor the attendance rates of a specific graduating class
  2. To quickly gather info on the attendance history of a particular student
  3. To effortlessly make contact with guidance counselors & parents

Architectural choices that keep the dashboard app extensible and manageable

  1. Monitoring state changes with ui-router to have complete control over where specific pieces of a web app are displayed. This allows for easy additon and removal of different application components
  2. Seperating business logic and persistent data from the controllers with services, factories and providers
  3. Testing to make bugs easier to find and fix
