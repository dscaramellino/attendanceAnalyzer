Attendance Analyzer

User Story:

This application is a teacher/principal facing dashboard that allows the user:

  1. To easily monitor the attendance rates of a specific graduating class
  2. To quickly gather information on the attendance history of a particular student
  3. To effortlessly make contact with guidance counselors/parents and quickly address student needs

Architectural choices that keep the dashboard extendable and manageable:

  1. Monitoring state changes with ui-router allowing complete control of how app components are displayed as well as allowing easy additon and removal
  2. Separating business logic and persistent data from the controllers with services, factories and providers - ie: separation of concerns
  3. Testing that makes bugs easier to locate and fix
