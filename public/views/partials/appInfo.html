About Attendance Analyzer

User Story:

This application is a teacher/principle facing dashboard that allows one :

  1. To easily monitor the attendance rates of a specific graduating class
  2. Quickly gather info on the attendance history of a particular student
  3. Effortlessly make contact with guidance counselors & parents

Architectural Choices/Changes to keep the dashboard app extensible and manageable

  1. Monitoring state Changes: ui-router gives complete control over where specific pieces of a web app are displayed. This allows for easy additon and removal of different application components
  2. Seperate business logic and persistent data from the controllers with services, factories and providers
  3. Implement testing to make bugs easier to find and fix
